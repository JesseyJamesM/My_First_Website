Some of My Favorite Types of Animals

As I said in my home page, I have a lot of cats. Currently my favorite big cat is a tiger, they just look so cool. I have a dog, he is a Saint Bernard, Black Lab, and maybe Great Pyrenees. At one time we have had 50 snakes, my first snake was a Short Tail Borneo Python, I named her Mousetrap. When we had the snakes we also started raising mice and rats, I liked the rats, they were fun to watch and play with, while the mice were to small to keep a hold of, and the snakes were nice to hold, but my favorite, Mousetrap, was heavy. She was around 3ft long I think.
